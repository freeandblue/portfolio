About me

I'm a research & development engineer for automotive electric machine with a focus on electromagnetic design and I love writing useful scripts to work clever and in a more efficient way.
Some of those scripts are for analytic calculation of electric machines, which helps to save time when you want to do small design variations and see, if the idea goes in the right direction. Of course it does not offer the accuracy as a FEA simulation, but it definitely helps for a quick idea check. I also have numerous small scripts that do simple calculations based on simple equations you need quite often as a motor design engineer. With growing number of scripts it became important for me to make those scripts accessable for me as a personal working assets. So I created this page as a database for all company/project neutral scripts including my hobby projects for web and mobile applications.
